Meet Our Founder

Ryan Lehning – Founder & CEO

Ryan Lehning is the visionary behind SentiNexus, where technology meets compassion. As a proud member of the U.S. Army, Ryan brings discipline, purpose, and a deep sense of duty to every project he undertakes. His passion for helping people and protecting animals fuels the core values of SentiNexus.

Beyond his military service, Ryan is a dedicated environmental advocate. He has led multiple research initiatives focused on pollution, river degradation, and sustainable restoration practices. These experiences have shaped his mission to integrate emotional intelligence and ecological awareness into the heart of AI innovation.

With a bold spirit and an unwavering commitment to positive impact, Ryan stands as the sole member of the SentiNexus team — for now. His story is just beginning, and the path forward is paved with empathy, integrity, and game-changing ideas.
