Scantivation GO Mission Statement

Scantivation GO is a groundbreaking mobile game from the SentiNexus family, designed to gamify environmental stewardship and inspire a new generation of eco-conscious explorers. Our mission is to bridge the digital and natural worlds by turning everyday outdoor experiences into opportunities for learning, conservation, and community impact.

Through real-world exploration, players engage with dynamic biomes, scan wildlife using augmented reality, and rescue virtual animals in need. Each interaction contributes to the growth of a personalized Conservation Park, where players build habitats, install sustainability upgrades, and create educational exhibits that reflect their journey and values.

Scantivation GO empowers players to:

  • Learn about biodiversity through interactive missions and eco-quizzes
  • Support real-world conservation via grant tokens and partnerships
  • Collaborate with others in EcoTeams to achieve global environmental goals
  • Contribute to citizen science by sharing anonymized wildlife data with researchers and nonprofits
